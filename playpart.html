Prudhvi Mohan Cherukupalli acts in a play

An Enemy of the People, by Henrik Ibsen

1st Citizen (meeting another).

Hullo, Lamstad! You here too?

2nd Citizen.

I go to every public meeting, I do.

3rd Citizen.

Brought your whistle too, I expect!

2nd Citizen.

I should think so. Haven't you?

3rd Citizen.

Rather! And old Evensen said he was going to bring a cow-horn, he did.

2nd Citizen.

Good old Evensen! (Laughter among the crowd.)

4th Citizen(coming up to them).

I say, tell me what is going on here tonight?
